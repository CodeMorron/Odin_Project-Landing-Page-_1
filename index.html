DISCOVER WYS

WYS has arrived in time to rescue winter with his album ‘South Pole’: a chilling, but warmly-welcomed sequel to the highly-acclaimed ‘North Pole’. Bundle up and grab your sled for this short, nostalgia-filled auditory trip. Comforting guitar riffs feel cosy as woollen gloves, and bittersweet melodies like icy serenades, together with soothing gusts of wind, will send you adrift into the endless expanse of white.

North Pole by the talented french beatmaker WYS sets the mood up for truly nostalgic and reminiscing vibes. Guaranteed to take you on a journey. Just close your eyes and drift away.
